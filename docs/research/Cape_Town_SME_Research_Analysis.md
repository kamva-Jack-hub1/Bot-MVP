## 🎯 **KEY CHALLENGES IDENTIFIED**

### **TOP 7 BARRIERS TO AI ADOPTION:**

1. **Limited Enterprise Maturity & Skills Gap** (68% of SMEs)
   - Lack internal AI skills and digital literacy
   - Difficulty understanding, implementing, maintaining AI solutions

2. **High Costs of AI Tools & Implementation**
   - Prohibitively expensive setup, training, maintenance
   - Tight budgets with unclear immediate ROI

3. **Poor Digital Infrastructure**
   - Unreliable internet connectivity (especially rural/semi-urban)
   - Outdated IT infrastructure
   - Power outages (load-shedding) affecting operations

4. **Low Awareness & Understanding of AI Benefits**
   - Limited knowledge about practical advantages
   - Low perceived usefulness
   - Adoption hesitation

5. **Cultural & Organizational Resistance**
   - Fear of disruption
   - Lack of absorptive capacity
   - Organizational culture barriers

6. **Limited Trialability & Observability**
   - Can't experiment before committing financially
   - Few visible local success stories
   - Reduced trust and confidence

7. **External Challenges**
   - Economic instability
   - Persistent power outages
   - Crime creating uncertain environment
